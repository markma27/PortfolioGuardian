About Us

PortfolioGuardian is a Chartered Accounting firm and investment administration & reporting specialist with over 20 years of experience managing investment portfolios. We also have deep expertise in supporting both Public and Private Ancillary Funds (PAF & PuAF), having helped more than 350 foundations achieve their philanthropic goals over the past decade.

We operate nationally and are recognised for delivering exceptional service and value. Unlike many platform providers, our fees are not based on the size of your portfolio - ensuring transparency and fairness.

Our team can administer any asset, on any platform and provide you with fully consolidated tax reporting. At year-end, we simplify your tax process by delivering a comprehensive summary of capital gains and losses - ready to hand over to your personal accountant, saving you both time and money.
