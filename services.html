PAF & PuAF Services

PortfolioGuardian offers specialized services for Private Ancillary Funds (PAFs) and Public Ancillary Funds (PuAFs). With over 17 years of experience, we understand the unique requirements and compliance obligations these structures face.

Our PAF & PuAF Services Include:

  • Establishment and ongoing administration of PAFs and PuAFs
  • Compliance monitoring and reporting
  • Management of minimum distribution requirements
  • Preparation of financial statements
  • Coordination with external auditors
  • Grant management and documentation
  • ATO lodgment assistance

We work closely with trustees to ensure all regulatory requirements are met while maximizing the philanthropic impact of your fund.

Annual Taxation Reports

Our comprehensive taxation reporting services are designed to make tax time straightforward and stress-free. We provide detailed, accurate reports that capture all the information needed for your tax returns.

Our Taxation Reporting Services Include:

  • Consolidated tax reports across all investments and platforms
  • Detailed breakdown of income types (dividends, interest, trust distributions, etc.)
  • Capital gains calculations with consideration of tax parcel selection methods
  • Tracking of tax credits, including franking credits and foreign tax paid
  • Reports formatted for easy handover to your accountant or tax advisor
  • Historical record-keeping for audit purposes

Our taxation reports are prepared with the utmost attention to detail, ensuring accuracy and compliance with current tax regulations.
